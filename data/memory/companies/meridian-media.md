# Meridian Media Group

## Company Overview

Meridian Media Group is a mid-size digital media and publishing company operating a portfolio of lifestyle, entertainment, and news properties. They reach approximately 45M unique monthly visitors across their owned-and-operated sites. Revenue is split 62% linear (print + events) / 38% digital (display, programmatic, sponsored content), with digital growing at 22% YoY.

## Strategic Priorities (2026)

1. **Accelerate digital revenue to 50% of total** — Board-level mandate to shift revenue mix
2. **Build first-party data capability** — Hired new CTO from Vox Media (Jan 2026) to lead tech investment
3. **Premium direct-sold tier** — Want to offer advertisers guaranteed, audience-targeted placements beyond standard programmatic
4. **Reduce advertiser churn** — Lost 3 top-20 advertisers in 2025 to competitors with better data offerings

## Key Pain Points (Validated)

1. **CPA inflation** — Programmatic CPA has risen 34% YoY, outpacing industry average of 12%. Estimated $1.8M additional cost in 2027 at current trajectory.
2. **Audience fragmentation** — First-party data covers only 40% of addressable audience. Remaining 60% relies on degrading third-party segments post-cookie deprecation.
3. **Competitive gap** — Three direct competitors (Dotdash Meredith, BDG, Condé Nast Digital) have invested in integrated data platforms in the last 18 months.
