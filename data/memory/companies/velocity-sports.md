# Velocity Sports Media

## Company Overview

Velocity Sports Media operates regional sports networks, a national streaming platform, and digital sports content properties. They hold broadcast rights for 4 NBA teams, 6 MLS teams, and college athletics conferences. Revenue splits roughly 45% carriage fees, 35% advertising, 20% streaming subscriptions. Total digital reach: 28M monthly uniques across web and app.

## Strategic Priorities (2026)

1. **Streaming monetization** — Launched DTC streaming in 2024, 2.1M subscribers. Need to grow ARPU through targeted advertising on the streaming tier.
2. **Cross-platform ad sales** — Advertisers want unified buys across linear, digital, and streaming. Currently sold through separate teams with separate systems.
3. **Sports betting partnerships** — Legalized sports betting is driving new advertiser demand. Need real-time ad serving and in-game sponsorship capabilities.
4. **Data unification** — Viewer data fragmented across linear (Nielsen), digital (GA4), and streaming (proprietary). No unified view of audience.

## Key Pain Points (Validated)

1. **Unified ad sales platform** — Three separate systems for selling linear, digital, and streaming. Sales team wastes 30% of time on manual reconciliation.
2. **Streaming ad fill rate** — Currently filling only 62% of available streaming ad inventory. Estimated $4.2M in unrealized annual revenue.
3. **Audience data silos** — Cannot offer cross-platform reach or frequency management. Advertisers seeing 8x+ frequency on some users while missing others entirely.

- Fast-moving organization accustomed to large technology investments
